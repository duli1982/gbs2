1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

First-Party Cookies

Set directly by our website and can only be read by us

Third-Party Cookies

Set by external services like Google Analytics

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device until expiry or manual deletion

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several important reasons:

Essential Functionality

Remember your login state, language preferences, and platform settings

Performance and Analytics

Understand how you use our platform to improve user experience

Personalization

Track your learning progress and customize content recommendations

Security

Protect against fraud and ensure secure platform access

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required

These cookies are essential for the platform to function and cannot be disabled. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or filling in forms.

Purpose: Session management, security, cookie consent preferences

Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest (essential for service delivery)

Analytics and Performance Cookies

Requires Consent

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use Google Analytics for this purpose.

Purpose: Usage analytics, page views, user behavior tracking

Legal Basis: Consent (via cookie banner)

Provider: Google Analytics

Functional Cookies

Optional

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and customizing your learning experience.

Purpose: User preferences, learning progress, personalization

Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest / Consent

7. How to Manage Cookies

You have several options to manage or disable cookies:

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our platform, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject optional cookies
  • Customize your preferences

Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

Important: If you disable or reject cookies, please note that some parts of the platform may become inaccessible or not function properly. Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for the platform to work.
